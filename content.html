Requirements:
  • A working installation of the Core module
  • 2 gigabytes of free disk space

The Goal of the Content Module:

The goal of this module is to add lore friendly and balanced new content to spice up the game for veteran players, and more great vanilla-feeling content to the game for beginner players. All mods are carefully picked out and tweaked to be as high quality and fun as possible.
