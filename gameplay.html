Requirements:

The Goal of the Gameplay Module:

The goal of this module is to make the game harder, but not impossible, yet more rewarding. All gameplay changes are carefully picked out and tweaked by myself to make them as balanced and vanilla-feeling as possible. It is designed to be played on either Normal/Hard difficulty with hardcore mode enabled so there won't be any bullet sponge enemies or major annoyances present.
